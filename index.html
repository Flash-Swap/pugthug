HOW TO BUY

The only place to buy or sell $PUGT safely is on Pancakeswap. $PUGT will also be listed in some centralized exchanges, but CEXs have a history of locking funds. If you can, it is safer to trade on a decentralized exchange like Pancakeswap or Biswap.

Download Trust Wallet.

Download Trust Wallet from Goolge Play for android and App Store for iOS users and create a wallet.
Keep your phrase a secret! Never share it with anyone and store it properly!

Add $PUGT to the Wallet.

Tap the icon in the top-right and search for "PugFather". If it's not there, tap "Add Custom Token".

At the top, tap "Ethereum" next to Network, and change it to "Smart Chain". Copy the contract address on this page and put it in the Contract Address box.

Next, put "PugFather" as the name, and the symbol as PUGT. Decimals will be 9.

Click "Done" at the top and you should now have PUGT added to your wallet.


Make sure to copy the contract address from below!
Address

Buy "Smart Chain" (BNB).

Tap on "Smart Chain" on the main screen of trust wallet, then tap "Buy" in the top right. This step may require KYC verification, so have documents ready to prove your identity.
If the transaction won't go through, you may need to contact your bank to allow international transactions.

After purchasing, there may be a delay while your transaction is processed. Be patient, this is normal!

Swap BSC for PugToken( $PUGT).

Once you have BNB on your Trust Wallet, Go to DApps (or "Browser" for iPhones) at the bottom of the main screen. If the Browser button is not visible at the bottom for iPhone, open Safari and in the URL type trust://browser_enable, then return to trust wallet.

Open the DApps, or Browser, section and find PancakeSwap and open it. Connect your Trust Wallet in the top-right. Scroll down a little to the "Exchange" box

Click the settings icon and set the slippage to 12%. If you want to give it the best possible change to clear, increase the deadline. By default it should be set to 20 minutes, which is fine. Swap BNB for PUGT.
